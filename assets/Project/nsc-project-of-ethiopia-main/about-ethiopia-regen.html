Afar

This region is predominantly desert and is famous for the Afar Depression and the Danakil Depression, one of the lowest and hottest places on Earth. It's also known for its active volcanoes and moon-like landscape, particularly the Erta Ale volcano. The Afar people, known for their resilience adapted to the harsh environment, are predominantly pastoralists.
